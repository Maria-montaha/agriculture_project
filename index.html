Crop Details

Learn about specific crop practices to improve yield, quality, and sustainability.
Explore strategies tailored to different crops for optimal farming results.

Wheat Farming

- **Optimal Season**: Winter crops grown from October to April.
- **Soil Requirements**: Well-drained loamy soil with pH 6.5-7.5.
- **Water Needs**: Moderate irrigation, critical at booting and flowering stages.
- **Fertilizer**: Balanced use of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium.
- **Yield**: Average 2.5-3 tons per hectare.
- **Pests**: Watch out for aphids, rust, and powdery mildew.

Rice Farming

- **Optimal Season**: Monsoon crop grown from June to November.
- **Soil Requirements**: Clayey soil with good water retention capacity.
- **Water Needs**: Requires standing water during the growth stage.
- **Fertilizer**: Apply urea and potash at tillering and panicle initiation.
- **Yield**: Average 3-4 tons per hectare.
- **Pests**: Monitor for stem borers, leaf folders, and blast disease.

Read More+
Corn Farming

- **Optimal Season**: Grows well in spring and summer (March to September).
- **Soil Requirements**: Fertile, well-drained sandy loam with pH 5.5-7.5.
- **Water Needs**: Requires moderate irrigation, especially during tasseling.
- **Fertilizer**: Nitrogen-heavy fertilizers applied at sowing and knee-height stages.
- **Yield**: Average 4-5 tons per hectare.

Pest Control in Farming

Effective pest management strategies to protect crops and boost productivity.
Learn about common pests and how to control them sustainably.

Wheat Pest Control

- **Aphids**: Use neem oil sprays or release ladybugs as natural predators.
- **Rust Disease**: Apply fungicides like Mancozeb at early stages.
- **Armyworms**: Use pheromone traps to
monitor and control infestations.
- **Prevention**: Rotate crops and use resistant wheat varieties.

Read More+
Rice Pest Control

- **Stem Borers**: Apply Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt) sprays or granular insecticides.
- **Leaf Folders**: Use light traps and biological agents like Trichogramma.
- **Blast Disease**: Use carbendazim fungicide or resistant varieties.
- **Prevention**: Keep fields flooded to drown pest eggs and larvae.

Read More+
Corn Pest Control

- **Corn Borers**: Introduce natural predators like parasitic wasps.
- **Armyworms**: Use insecticide-treated seeds and rotate crops.
- **Fungal Diseases**: Apply fungicides like Azoxystrobin or Thiophanate-methyl.
- **Prevention**: Practice no-till farming to reduce pest habitats.
